How To Search Weather

  1. Type a city name
  2. Click "Get Weather"
  3. Results will Appear

Understanding Weather Advice

Weather Helper provides simple recommendations based on weather conditions

  • Rain = bring an umbrella
  • Cold temperatures = dress warmly
  • Hot temperatures = Stay hydrated

Saved Locations

  • Users can save cities for quick access later
  • Use the "Save Location" button after searching a city
  • Saved cities appear in the sidebar and can be refreshed or cleared

Temperature Chart

The temperature chart displays the current temperature, feels-like temperature, minimum and maximum temperature for selected city

City Map

The city map shows the location of the searched city using an itneractive map
